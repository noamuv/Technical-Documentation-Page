Introduction

Fashion is an important aspect in our society, first impressions count & the clothes we wear can say a lot about who we are as a person.

  1. Personality
  2. Beliefs
  3. Cultural Heritage
  4. Social status

This documentation will teach you the foundations of styling yourself that you can apply in your everyday life. That being proportions. However, when talking about proportions, two other aspects are important to take into consideration:

  • Silhouette
  • Body Type
Silhouettes
                    "The overall shape that the clothing you are wearing makes up as it sits on your body"

Think of a silhouette as the shadow of your outfit

Proportions
                    "How lines & shapes divide up the sections of an outfit into different parts"

It involves how the parts of an outfit look in relationship to one another.

e.g. A tight t-shirt + wide trousers

Thus, creating a particular proportion on the top half & bottom half which creates an overall silhouette.

                    "The correlation between proportions and silhouettes is that proportions makes the silhouette"
Rule of 3rds

The rule of 3rds is a borrowed concept from architecture, it states:

                    "The most visually appealing division of spaces in architecture is when you have a 1/3 to 2/3 ratio"
Body Type

When discussing fashion, it is important to take ones body type into consideration.

                        "The dress must follow the body of a woman. Not the body following the shape of a dress."
